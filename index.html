Kolhapur is a historic city in Maharashtra known for its temples, palaces, lakes, and rich cultural heritage. It is famous for the Mahalaxmi Temple, Kolhapuri chappals, spicy cuisine, and vibrant festivals.

Highlights of Kolhapur

  • Mahalaxmi Temple – iconic and spiritual center.
  • New Palace – beautiful blend of history and architecture.
  • Kolhapuri Cuisine – fiery and flavorful delights.
  • Kolhapuri Chappals – handcrafted leather footwear.
